About

Indigenous stories have been told and shared through many generations.

Often given in dreams, and moments of clarity. Our forefathers heard these stories and with sacrifice and courage forged a new life, in a land undiscovered.

As generations past, many stopped hearing, stopped dreaming, stopped believing, stopped understanding.

Our ancestors have emerged again, to connect to those who can hear and share their stories once more.

iwibooks.com does not represent traditional indigenous tales but embraces new indigenous stories depicting connections, remembrance, community and family.
