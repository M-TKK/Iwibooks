When Will I See My Koro Again?

Author: Lisa Eden

Illustrator: Deni Nugroho

ISBN: 978-0-6483714-1-0

Format: Hardcover | 40 Pages | PictureBook

Language: Bilingual | Maori | English

Dimensions" 8.5x0.2x11 in

Weight: 0.39Kg

Age Range: 4-8 Years Old

Catagory: General Fiction | Friendship | Bereavement

Series: A Maori Boy called Tama

Description

This is a story about the journey of a young Maori boy, who is dealing with the loss of his beloved Grandfather. With the help of his farm animal friends, his dog, and an eel, he experiences friendship, adventure, understanding and wisdom.
A heartwarming picture book with themes of friendship, adventure and remembrance. Beautifully illustrated with rural inspired landscapes.
This edition is bilingual with Maori and English words. Included is a glossary of Maori words with English translation.
